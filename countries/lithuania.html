Vilnius

City Information: Vilnius, the capital of Lithuania, is famous for its baroque architecture, historic Old Town, and vibrant cultural scene.

Tour Description:
Immerse yourself in the historic and cultural heart of Vilnius.

Old Town: Walk through the UNESCO World Heritage site with stops at Gediminas Tower and the Vilnius Cathedral.
Uzupis District: Visit the bohemian area with its unique art and culture.
Vilnius University: Tour one of the oldest universities in Eastern Europe.
The Gates of Dawn: See the revered religious icon and city gate.

Expect: Baroque architecture, rich history, and vibrant cultural districts.

Trakai

City Information: Trakai is known for its beautiful island castle and picturesque lakes.

Tour Description:
Explore the enchanting town of Trakai.

Trakai Island Castle: Tour the iconic 14th-century castle located on an island in Lake Galve.
Karaim Ethnographic Museum: Learn about the Karaim community and their culture.
Trakai Historical National Park: Enjoy the natural beauty and serene environment.

Expect: Medieval history, cultural heritage, and scenic landscapes.
